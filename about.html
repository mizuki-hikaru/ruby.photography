Hi, I’m Ruby, a photographer with a passion for capturing the beauty in everyday moments. Whether I’m wandering through quiet forests, observing city life, or photographing people in natural light, my goal is always the same: to tell a story through the lens.

I’m drawn to details that are often overlooked—fleeting expressions, subtle textures, and the quiet interplay of light and shadow. This portfolio is a collection of some of my favorite work. Thanks for stopping by.
